# Stackly POS - Product Overview

## Project Purpose
Stackly POS is a comprehensive Point of Sale (POS) system designed for businesses to manage their operations efficiently. It provides a complete business management solution with modules for inventory, customer relationship management, sales, and user administration.

## Value Proposition
- **All-in-one Business Management**: Combines POS functionality with inventory management, customer tracking, and business analytics
- **Multi-user Support**: Role-based access control with different permission levels (Super Admin, Admin, User)
- **Data Import/Export**: Excel-based bulk import capabilities for products and customers
- **Email Integration**: Automated email notifications and OTP verification system
- **PDF Generation**: Professional quotations and delivery notes with PDF export
- **Security-focused**: Input validation, SQL injection prevention, and session management

## Key Features

### Authentication & User Management
- Secure login/logout with session timeout
- OTP-based email verification for signup
- Password reset functionality via email
- Role-based access control (Super Admin, Admin, User)
- User profile management

### Masters Management
- **Product Master**: Complete product catalog with categories, pricing, stock levels
- **Customer Master**: Customer database with contact details, credit limits, and types
- **Department & Roles**: Organizational structure management
- **User Management**: Create and manage system users with different roles

### CRM (Customer Relationship Management)
- **Enquiry Management**: Track customer inquiries and follow-ups
- **Quotation System**: Generate professional quotations with email delivery
- **Customer Communication**: Email integration for customer correspondence

### Sales & Billing
- **Quick Billing**: Fast checkout process for retail transactions
- **Sales Orders**: Manage customer orders and fulfillment
- **Delivery Notes**: Generate delivery documentation

### Data Management
- **Bulk Import**: Excel template-based import for products and customers
- **Data Validation**: Comprehensive validation during import processes
- **Export Capabilities**: Download templates and export data

## Target Users

### Primary Users
- **Small to Medium Businesses**: Retail stores, service providers, distributors
- **Multi-location Businesses**: Companies with multiple branches or warehouses
- **B2B Companies**: Businesses requiring quotation and order management

### User Roles
- **Super Admin**: Full system access, user management, system configuration
- **Admin**: Business operations, data management, reporting
- **User**: Day-to-day operations, sales processing, customer service

## Use Cases

### Retail Operations
- Process customer sales quickly with Quick Billing
- Manage inventory levels and reorder points
- Track customer purchase history and preferences

### B2B Sales
- Generate professional quotations for business customers
- Manage customer credit limits and payment terms
- Track sales orders from inquiry to delivery

### Multi-branch Management
- Centralized user and role management across locations
- Department-wise organization structure
- Branch-specific inventory and sales tracking

### Data Migration
- Import existing product catalogs from Excel
- Migrate customer databases with validation
- Bulk setup of organizational structure

## Business Benefits
- **Operational Efficiency**: Streamlined processes from inquiry to delivery
- **Data Accuracy**: Validation and duplicate prevention during data entry
- **Customer Service**: Complete customer history and communication tracking
- **Scalability**: Multi-user, multi-branch capable architecture
- **Compliance**: Proper data validation and security measures
